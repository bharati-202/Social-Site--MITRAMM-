Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the registration process.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your email.

Can I change my username?

Yes, you can change your username in your account settings.

How is my data protected?

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data.

Who can see my posts?

You can control the visibility of your posts in your privacy settings.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Click the three dots (...) on any post and select "Report" to flag inappropriate content.

How do I add friends?

Search for users and click the "Add Friend" button on their profile.

Can I customize my profile?

Yes, you can customize your profile picture, cover photo, and bio in your profile settings.

How do I create a group?

Go to the Groups section and click "Create Group" to start a new group.

What should I do if I can't log in?

Try resetting your password or contact our support team for assistance.

Why can't I upload photos?

Check that your file meets our size and format requirements. If issues persist, try clearing your browser cache.

How do I contact support?

Visit our Contact page or email support@socialnetwork.com for assistance.
